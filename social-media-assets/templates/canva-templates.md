## ğŸ¯ Quick Design Tips

1. **Less is more** - Don't overcrowd slides
2. **One idea per slide** - Keep focused
3. **Use icons** - Emojis work great
4. **High contrast** - Dark bg + light text or vice versa
5. **Consistent spacing** - Use Canva's alignment tools
6. **CTA on every post** - Save, Share, Follow, Comment
